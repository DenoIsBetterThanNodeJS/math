Multiplication

Multiplication is a mathematical operation that involves combining equal groups to find the total number of items. For example, if you have 4 groups of 3 apples each, you multiply 4 by 3 to find out that you have 12 apples in total. The symbol for multiplication is the asterisk (*) or sometimes a dot (·). Multiplication is used in many real-life situations, such as when you are calculating the total cost of multiple items, determining the area of a rectangle, or figuring out how many items are in several groups. It’s a powerful tool for understanding and solving problems involving repeated addition.
