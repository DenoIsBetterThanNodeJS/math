Addition

Addition is one of the basic operations in mathematics. It involves combining two or more numbers to get a total or sum. For example, if you have 3 apples and you get 2 more, you can add them together to find out that you now have 5 apples. The symbol for addition is the plus sign (+). Addition is used in everyday life, such as when you are counting money, measuring ingredients for a recipe, or keeping track of scores in a game. It's a fundamental skill that helps us understand and solve many different types of problems.
